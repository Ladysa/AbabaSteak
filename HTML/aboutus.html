ABABA STEAK'S HISTORY
ABABA Steak was opened by Mr. Ababa and his wife back in 1999. We are the first and the best steakhouse that opened in Yogyakarta. Ababa steak is widely known by the locals, even known by outsiders, for its wide variety of food, reasonable price, and uniqueness in taste.
ABABA Steak was closed during the year 2002-2003 for some reason. But, with the people's love of our quality of steak, ABABA Steak decided to re-open their new steakhouse in South Jakarta.
ABABA Steak is committed to serving the customers as kings and queens that understand what it meant to taste quality grilled meat. With centuries-old recipes and techniques, ABABA Steak strives to serve the outmost perfect steak using fresh and natural ingredients.
